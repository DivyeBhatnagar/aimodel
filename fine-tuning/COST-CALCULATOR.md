# Fine-Tuning Cost Calculator

## Hardware Options

### Option 1: Your Mac (FREE)
- **Cost:** $0
- **Time:** 8-12 hours (CPU) or 4-6 hours (GPU)
- **Pros:** Free, private, no setup
- **Cons:** Slow, ties up your computer

### Option 2: Google Colab Free (FREE)
- **Cost:** $0
- **Time:** 4-6 hours
- **Pros:** Free GPU, no local resources
- **Cons:** Session limits (12 hours max)
- **Link:** https://colab.research.google.com

### Option 3: Google Colab Pro ($10/month)
- **Cost:** $10/month subscription
- **Time:** 2-4 hours
- **Pros:** Better GPUs, longer sessions
- **Cons:** Monthly subscription

### Option 4: RunPod (PAY-PER-USE)
- **Cost:** $0.69-$1.89/hour
- **Time:** 2-3 hours
- **Total:** $1.38-$5.67
- **Pros:** Fast, flexible, no commitment
- **Link:** https://runpod.io

### Option 5: Lambda Labs (PAY-PER-USE)
- **Cost:** $1.10-$1.50/hour
- **Time:** 2-3 hours
- **Total:** $2.20-$4.50
- **Pros:** Fast, reliable
- **Link:** https://lambdalabs.com

### Option 6: AWS/GCP (ENTERPRISE)
- **Cost:** $3-10/hour
- **Time:** 2-3 hours
- **Total:** $6-30
- **Pros:** Scalable, production-ready
- **Cons:** Complex setup, expensive

## Cost Optimization Tips

1. **Start with free options** - Test before paying
2. **Batch your training** - Collect data, train monthly
3. **Use spot instances** - 50-70% cheaper on cloud
4. **Train overnight** - Use your Mac while you sleep
5. **Share GPU costs** - Split with team members
